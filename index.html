A Recognition

Many forms of healing help us suffer less.

But eventually another question appears: What if the struggle itself is being created by the mind trying to escape experience?

The end of seeking is not the end of growth. It is the end of seeing problems that need solving, in everyone, everything and in yourself.

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Athena Nhan Ta

After seventeen years of spiritual seeking, meditation practice, psychological training, and lived integration, Athena developed this contemporary framework for understanding how suffering is created — and how it naturally dissolves when addressed at its roots.
